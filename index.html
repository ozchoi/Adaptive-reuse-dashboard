Suitability Model for Industrial-to-Residential Adaptive Reuse in Hong Kong

A research workflow dashboard linking literature-derived critical factors, survey preparation, stakeholder input, final weights and adaptive reuse suitability modelling.

© 2026 Chun Yin Oz Choi. Dashboard developed for the research project “Optimized decision-making models for facilitating adaptive reuse of vacant industrial buildings for residential use in Hong Kong,” led by Prof. Ruffina Thilakaratne, Department of Architecture, Chu Hai College of Higher Education.
