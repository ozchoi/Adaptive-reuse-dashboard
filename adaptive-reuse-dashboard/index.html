Hong Kong Industrial Building Reuse Suitability Dashboard

An indicative decision-support tool for screening industrial-to-residential adaptive reuse opportunities.

This dashboard provides a prototype decision-support model for identifying vacant or underused industrial buildings with potential for residential adaptive reuse in Hong Kong. Suitability scores combine planning, lease, ownership, safety, building adaptability, transport, district capacity, neighbourhood compatibility, economic feasibility and policy implementation factors. Scores are intended for early-stage screening only and should be verified through site inspection, statutory planning review, lease assessment, fire safety audit, environmental screening and cost feasibility study.

Academic research prototype. Outputs are indicative and subject to verification.

Indicative screening
